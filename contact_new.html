Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

How do I book a Hajj or Umrah package?

You can book by contacting us directly through this form, calling our office, or visiting us in person. We'll guide you through the available packages and booking process.

What documents do I need for Hajj or Umrah?

Typically, you'll need a valid passport with at least 6 months validity, passport-sized photos, completed application forms, and sometimes a mahram certificate for women.

How far in advance should I book?

For Hajj, we recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance as spots fill quickly. For Umrah, 2-3 months notice is usually sufficient, though Ramadan packages should be booked earlier.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special rates for groups of 10 or more pilgrims. Please contact our office directly to discuss group package options and pricing.
