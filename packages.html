Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my Hajj package?

We recommend booking your Hajj package 8-12 months in advance to ensure availability, as spots fill up quickly due to country quotas.

Can I customize my package after booking?

Yes, certain aspects of your package can be modified after booking, depending on availability. Please contact our team for specific requests.

What payment options do you offer?

We offer flexible payment plans including installment options. Typically, a deposit is required at booking with full payment due 2-3 months before departure.

Are flights included in your packages?

Yes, All the packages include flights to Jeddah and return flight to India. This is clearly indicated in the package description. We can also arrange Seperate Business Class flights upon request but not economy class.
