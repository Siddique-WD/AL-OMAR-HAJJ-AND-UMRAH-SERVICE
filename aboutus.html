Our Story

Al-Omar Hajj and Umrah Services was established in 2017 with a mission to provide pilgrims with a spiritually fulfilling and comfortable journey to the holy cities of Makkah and Madinah.

Our founder, Sadiq Ahmed, started this company after performing Hajj himself and noticing the challenges that many pilgrims faced. He envisioned a service that would make the sacred journey easier, allowing pilgrims to focus entirely on their worship and spiritual growth.

Over the years, Al Omar Hajj and Umrah Services has grown into a reputable brand, serving thousands of pilgrims from around the world. Despite our growth, we maintain our commitment to personalized service and attention to detail that sets us apart.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional Hajj and Umrah services that make the sacred journey comfortable, meaningful, and hassle-free, allowing pilgrims to focus on their spiritual experience and worship.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and respected provider of Hajj and Umrah services in Tamil Nadu, known for our excellence, integrity, and dedication to the spiritual well-being of our pilgrims.
